Study Spaces at UM

Find Your Perfect Study Spot

UM offers a variety of study spaces to help you focus and succeed academically. Whether you're looking for a quiet corner or a collaborative space, you'll find it on campus.

Libraries

UM has several libraries with study rooms and quiet areas perfect for deep work:

  • Hatcher Graduate Library: Quiet spaces and group study rooms available for reservation.
  • Shapiro Undergraduate Library: Open 24 hours with a mix of quiet and collaborative spaces.
  • Duderstadt Center: Ideal for engineering and art students, offering creative spaces and study rooms.

North Quad

Located in the central campus, North Quad offers a comfortable and vibrant atmosphere for both individual and group study.

Other Study Spaces

  • Trotter Multicultural Center: A welcoming space for students of all backgrounds, providing study areas and resources.
  • Ross School of Business: Comfortable spaces for group work and individual study, including the Kresge Library.
  • Law Library: Known for its quiet atmosphere and impressive architecture, this is a great place for focused study sessions.

Reserve a Study Room

You can reserve a study room at any of these locations to ensure a quiet, focused space for your academic needs.
